      How many Cross River gorillas are left?
      200-300 Cross River Gorillas
      The Cross River Gorilla is a subspecies of the Western Gorilla. There are currently only 200-300 Cross River Gorillas left in the wild, making them the most endangered great ape in Africa.

      How long do Cross River gorillas live?
      35 to 50 years
      Cross River gorillas stand 4.7 to 5.5 feet (1.4 to 1.7m) tall and weigh between 220 and 440 pounds (100 to 200kg). Lifespan in the wild is 35 to 50 years.
